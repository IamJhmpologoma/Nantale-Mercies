About Nantale Mercies

Building a brighter future for Uganda's children through holistic care and support.

✨ Our Mission

Our mission is to build a brighter Uganda where every child—no matter their background or circumstances—has equal access to quality education, reliable healthcare, and meaningful opportunities to thrive. We believe that every child deserves a fair chance to reach their full potential, and we are committed to breaking barriers that keep vulnerable children from living healthy, fulfilled, and empowered lives.

🎯 Our Objectives

🌱

To Provide Essential Support

We work to meet the immediate needs of vulnerable children by providing nutritious food, safe shelter, quality education, and life-saving medical care.

📚

To Empower Through Education

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. We are dedicated to improving access to education and equipping children with the skills they need to succeed.

❤️

To Improve Health Outcomes

We believe that a healthy child is a hopeful child. We strive to ensure access to quality healthcare services and promote preventive health practices.

💼

To Promote Sustainable Livelihoods

We support parents and guardians through income-generating projects and vocational training that foster self-reliance and long-term stability.

🛡️

To Advocate for Children's Rights

We raise awareness and work with policymakers to defend and protect children's rights, creating an environment where every child is safe and respected.

🙏

God Bless You

Together, we can transform lives and build a future filled with hope, dignity, and opportunity for Uganda's children.

Survivor Stories

Real stories of hope, resilience, and transformation.

Sarah's Journey to School

"Before Nantale Mercies, I didn't think I would ever wear a school uniform. Today, I am top of my class."

Sarah came to us at age 8, malnourished and out of school. Through our education sponsorship program, she not only enrolled in school but received nutritional support that helped her thrive academically.

A Mother's New Beginning

"The vocational training changed my life. I can now feed my children and pay their school fees."

Grace is a single mother of three. Through our sustainable livelihood program, she received training in tailoring and a startup kit. She now runs a successful business in her community.
